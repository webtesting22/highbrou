The Problem
75 coupling beams — each packed with 527 kg diagonal steel — so congested that site workers could not thread the rebar. Work had stopped. The pile raft was also adding enormous cost in reclamation soil under a 9m deep basement.
What We Did
  • Pile-free raft — joint analysis confirmed no piles needed, 5–7% substructure saving and ~3 months recovered.
  • Composite coupling beams — 527 kg to 62 kg per beam (88% less congestion) across all 75 locations — ~2 months RCC time saved.
  • Simplified slab detailing — 1 day saved per floor across 24+ levels — ~1 month recovered, less bank interest, less tenant rent.
